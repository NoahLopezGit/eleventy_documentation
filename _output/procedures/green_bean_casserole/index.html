Ingredients:

  1. 1 pound green beans, trimmed and cut into 1-inch pieces
  2. 2 tablespoons butter
  3. 1 small onion, finely chopped
  4. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  5. 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
  6. 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  7. 1 cup chicken or vegetable broth
  8. 1 cup heavy cream
  9. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  10. 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  11. 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
  12. 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  13. 1 1/2 cups crispy fried onions

Procedure

Step # Description Expected Measured Sign-Off

Step 1

 Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

Step 2

 Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the green beans and cook for 5 minutes until they are slightly tender. Drain the beans and immediately transfer them to a bowl of ice water to stop the cooking process. Drain again and set aside.

5 minutes

Step 3

 In a large skillet or sauté pan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and sauté for about 2 minutes until they become translucent and fragrant.

2 minutes

Step 4

 Add the sliced mushrooms to the skillet and cook for another 5 minutes until they release their moisture and start to brown. Stir occasionally.

5 minutes

Step 5

 Sprinkle the flour over the mushrooms and onions, and stir well to coat. Cook for about 1 minute to cook off the raw flour taste.

1 minute

Step 6

 Slowly pour in the chicken or vegetable broth while stirring continuously. Allow the mixture to simmer for a few minutes until it thickens slightly.

Step 7

 Add the heavy cream, salt, black pepper, and dried thyme to the skillet. Stir everything together and let it simmer for another 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens further.

Step 8

 Remove the skillet from heat and stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until it melts and incorporates into the sauce.

2-3 minutes

Step 9

 Add the blanched green beans to the skillet and toss them gently to coat them with the sauce.

Step 10

 Transfer the green bean mixture to a 2-quart casserole dish, spreading it out evenly.

Step 11

 Sprinkle the crispy fried onions on top of the green bean mixture, covering it completely.

Step 12

 Place the casserole dish in the preheated oven and bake for about 25 minutes or until the casserole is bubbling and the fried onions turn golden brown.

25 minutes

Step 13

 Once cooked, remove the casserole from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving
