Chilli Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 1 pound of ground beef (or other protein of choice, e.g., ground turkey, tofu)
  2. 1 large onion, diced
  3. 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  4. 1 bell pepper, diced
  5. 1 can of kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  6. 1 can of diced tomatoes
  7. 2 tablespoons of chili powder
  8. 1 teaspoon of cumin
  9. 1 teaspoon of oregano
  10. 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  11. 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  12. 1 cup of beef broth (or vegetable broth for a vegetarian option)
  13. Olive oil
  14. Optional toppings: shredded cheese, sour cream, chopped green onions

 Prepare your ingredients: Dice the onion and bell pepper, mince the garlic, and rinse the kidney beans.

 Heat the oil: In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat a tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat.

Step 3

 Cook the onions and bell pepper: Add the diced onion and bell pepper to the pot. Saut√© until the onion becomes translucent, about 5 minutes.

Step 4

 Add garlic and ground beef: Add the minced garlic and ground beef to the pot. Break up the beef with a spoon and cook until no pink remains, about 7-10 minutes.

Step 5

 Add spices: Sprinkle the chili powder, cumin, oregano, salt, and black pepper over the cooked beef. Stir well to coat the meat in the spices.

Step 6

 Add tomatoes and broth: Pour the diced tomatoes (with their juice) and beef broth into the pot. Stir well.

Step 7

 Simmer: Lower the heat to medium-low and let your chili simmer. Cover the pot and let it cook for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Step 8

 Add beans: After the chili has been simmering for 30 minutes, stir in the kidney beans.

Step 9

 Cook a little longer: Continue to let the chili simmer for another 15 minutes. This will help the flavors meld together and the beans to warm up.

15 Min Cook Time

Step 10

 Serve and enjoy: Once the chili is done cooking, ladle it into bowls and serve with your favorite toppings like shredded cheese, sour cream, or chopped green onions.

Green Bean Casserole Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 1 pound green beans, trimmed and cut into 1-inch pieces
  2. 2 tablespoons butter
  3. 1 small onion, finely chopped
  4. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  5. 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
  6. 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  7. 1 cup chicken or vegetable broth
  8. 1 cup heavy cream
  9. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  10. 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  11. 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
  12. 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  13. 1 1/2 cups crispy fried onions

Step 3

Step 4

 Add the sliced mushrooms to the skillet and cook for another 5 minutes until they release their moisture and start to brown. Stir occasionally.

Step 5

 Sprinkle the flour over the mushrooms and onions, and stir well to coat. Cook for about 1 minute to cook off the raw flour taste.

Step 6

 Slowly pour in the chicken or vegetable broth while stirring continuously. Allow the mixture to simmer for a few minutes until it thickens slightly.

25 minutes

Step 13

 Once cooked, remove the casserole from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving

Teriyaki Chicken Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  2. 1/2 cup soy sauce
  3. 1/4 cup water
  4. 1/4 cup brown sugar
  5. 2 tablespoons honey
  6. 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  7. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  8. 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  9. 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  10. 2 tablespoons water
  11. 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
Step # Description Expected Measured Sign-Off

Step 1

 In a mixing bowl, combine soy sauce, water, brown sugar, honey, rice vinegar, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Stir well until the sugar is dissolved.

Step 2

 Place the chicken breasts in a resealable plastic bag and pour half of the teriyaki sauce mixture over the chicken. Seal the bag and marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes, or up to overnight. Reserve the remaining sauce for later.

Step 3

 In a small bowl, whisk together cornstarch and water to create a slurry.

Step 4

 Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat.

Step 5

 Remove the chicken from the marinade and discard the marinade. Cook the chicken in the preheated pan for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until cooked through and browned. The cooking time may vary depending on the thickness of the chicken breasts.

Step 6

 While the chicken is cooking, transfer the reserved teriyaki sauce to a small saucepan. Bring it to a boil over medium heat.

Step 7

 Add the cornstarch slurry to the boiling sauce, stirring constantly. Continue cooking for 1-2 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened to your desired consistency.

Step 8

 Once the chicken is cooked through, remove it from the heat and let it rest for a few minutes.

Step 9

 Slice the cooked chicken into thin strips or bite-sized pieces.

Step 10

 Pour the thickened teriyaki sauce over the cooked chicken, ensuring that all the pieces are coated.

Step 11

 Garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions, if desired.
